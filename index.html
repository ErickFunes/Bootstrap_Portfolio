About Myself

Hello there, my name is Erick Funes. I was born Sept. 8, 1993 5:35P.M. in SF General. I can thank my parents for my Salvadorian background. My parents migrated here to America for a better future & decided having me all at the same time in their 20's. In my spare time I like to hang out with my girlfriend, family, & friends(In that order). I enjoy boxing & working out in my gym garage. NOTHING BEATS BEING OUTDOORS. I love being in nature & exploring. Fast cars is a plus with my heavy foot problem.

There were a few factors that got me captivated in the code world & into website development. First was I wanted to have a skill that I know can give me leverage in a busniess so I can be part of a main vein instead of just working for them. I then ran into Weebly, a website template builder. I started getting the hang of the program & got confident enough to promote myself as a web designer. Later on I found someone who needed my services & got paid $50/hr for the website until it was completed. I knew after that this is what my leverage will be. After that I ran into online code courses like Khan Academy, Code Academy, & so on to build my skills more & learn to code like HTML. I then ran into codify academy which is helping me learn HTML, CSS, BOOTSTRAP, JAVASCRIPT, JQUERY, & much more in depth.

My future plans are to become a self-started business owner or co-owner of future companies that will need me for business decisions & maintaining the web aspect of it. I also want to create games, apps, & websites that I will need to keep maintained threw independent contracting.

There you have it! You know now where I started, where I'm at, & where I'm going!
